Featured Student

Alan Yeoh

Meet Alan Yeoh, an inspiring student who has been part of our Arthematic visual art program since he was 11. Born deaf, Alan joined our program initially to explore visual art, and over time, at the request of his parents, he ventured into digital art. In fact, our digital art curriculum was developed with him in mind. Alan has turned what some might see as a limitation into his greatest strength, demonstrating unwavering focus and dedication to his craft. With the support of his mother, Alan has now established his own art studio and is thriving as a professional illustrator. Skilled in both hand-drawn and digital artwork, he brings any subject to life with remarkable detail. We couldn’t be prouder of Alan’s achievements and the incredible artist he has become.
