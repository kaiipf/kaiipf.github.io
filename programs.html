Our Programs

One Program to Engage All

Arthematic differs from other institutions with its unique All-In-One Program for all students. The Arthematic All-in-One Program is a comprehensive, engaging program designed to immerse children in a dynamic range of art disciplines. Combining visual arts, clay model making, comic storyboarding, STEAM learning, digital arts, and fine arts, Arthematic creates a well-rounded curriculum that cultivates creativity, problem-solving, and a love for discovery. Each subject is seamlessly integrated to develop artistic skills alongside scientific understanding, fostering both imagination and innovation. This program empowers children to explore various art forms, experiment with techniques, and build a strong foundation across the spectrum of arts and creative sciences.

Program Activities

ArThematic's unique programming explores a new topic each month, ranging from exciting topics in history, geography, science and many more. Each month, children engage with these subjects through a structured approach built on three core pillars: Thematics, Inspiration and Innovation. This approach ensures a rich, well-rounded learning experience that sparks curiosity, encourages creativity, and fosters innovative thinking.

Thematic Month

During Thematic Month, we focus on integrating drawing, crafting, storyboarding, and presenting into a unified study flow, with our goal being to guide children through the creative transformation of a themed idea into a fully realized project, whilst also learning about the world around them. Throughout this process, your child will explore various aspects of creative design, ultimately mastering skills such as visual illustration, 3D modeling, and effective storyboarding for presentations. By the end of the program, they will have developed a comprehensive project that showcases their creativity, technical skills, and storytelling abilities.

Inspiration Month

Inspiration Month is designed to unlock your child's self-expression through the transformative power of art. Whether they are just beginning or already experienced, this program equips them with essential techniques that foster creativity and personal expression. By the end of the program, your child will have discovered new and meaningful ways to communicate through art, gaining confidence and developing their unique creative voice.

Innovation Month

Our Innovation Month is aimed on helping your child discover the wonderful world of both art and science. Through our themed hands-on projects and more, this program encourages your child to experiment and explore the world around them, igniting that spark of curiosity in your child. This program will bring out the full extent of your child's creative mind and inspire them to become an innovative leader.
